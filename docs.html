Getting Started

Introduction to Awadh DAO

Awadh DAO is a decentralized autonomous organization built on principles of community ownership, transparency, and collaborative decision-making. This documentation will help you understand how to participate and contribute.

Core Principles

  • Open participation - anyone can join and contribute
  • Transparency - all decisions and discussions are public
  • Decentralization - no single point of authority
  • Meritocracy - contributions are valued based on their quality

How to Join

Step 1: Create Your Profile

Visit our community channels (Discord, Twitter) and introduce yourself. Tell us about your interests and skills.

Step 2: Explore Opportunities

Check out active projects and initiatives. Look for areas where you can contribute based on your expertise.

Step 3: Start Contributing

Begin with small tasks or discussions. As you get more involved, you can take on larger responsibilities.

First Steps as a Contributor

  • Join our Discord server for real-time community interaction
  • Read our governance documents and understand how decisions are made
  • Attend community meetings and workshops
  • Identify areas where you want to contribute

Voting & Governance

Understanding Proposals

Proposals are formal requests for community approval on specific actions, changes, or initiatives. Every community member can submit a proposal.

Voting Process

  • Proposals are posted with a clear description and timeline
  • Community members review and discuss the proposal
  • Voting occurs on-chain for transparency
  • Results are published and implemented if approved

Frequently Asked Questions

What do I need to join?

You only need an interest in Web3, culture, and community building. No prior experience required!

How do I get rewarded for contributions?

Contributions are recognized and rewarded based on community consensus. Rewards can include tokens, recognition, or other forms of compensation.

Can I propose ideas?

Absolutely! Anyone can propose ideas. Simply follow the proposal format and submit it to the community for discussion and voting.
